How to make a PB and J sandwich

  1. Get 2 slices of bread
  2. Get peanut butter
  3. Get jelly
  4. Get 2 knives
  5. Use the first knife to put peanut butter on the first slice of bread
  6. Use the second knife to put jelly in the second piece of bread
  7. Stick the 2 slices together
  8. Enjoy!
